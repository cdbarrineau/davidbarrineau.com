About

I'm a Senior Architect working for a firm out of Orlando Florida. I've worked for this company for over 20 years on mainly US DoD contracts that help train the military for battle by qualifying Marines and Soldiers in everything from Small Arms to tanks to aviation. Before that I worked for the Kennedy Space Center in Florida on software to replace the existing system to launch the Space Shuttle.

I have held multiple roles during the past 25 years for software engineering, from junior through senior roles and currently an architect.

I hold multiple certifications ranging from Soft Skills to Agile to AWS Solutions Architect - Professional.
